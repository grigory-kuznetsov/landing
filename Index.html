What we offer at SIHA

Ice Hockey Games

SIHA offers its members diverse sets of divisions accoridng to skill level to provide the most enjoyable playing experience. Whether you play in the NIHL or just in the shinny's, your game experience is optimised by the detailed organisation and availability of facilities.

Ice Hockey Training Sessions

As a member of SIHA, you are able to jon training sessions from strength workouts to skating and shooting drills. This allows you to develop your hockey skills from different aspects as much as you need.

Ice Hockey Officiating

If you are a hockey enthusiast and want to enhance your presence in the Ice Hockey community in Singapore, you can join the SIHA officiating team and seminars in order to finalize your spot as a referee in the NIHL and other junior leagues in Singapore - you can even go as far as refereeing IIHF events.
